OVERVIEW
Tattoos are permanent. Normally, it takes a while for people to decide the patterns, colors and size of the tattoos they want to have. To some degree, tattoos are like a spokesman that represent people's attitude to this world.
MY CONCEPT
It's really hard to tell whether people have tattoos when they are fully covered with clothes. Meanwhile, it's pretty fun to take a guess, especially when they are stone faced. I asked my models not to make any face expressions. I like the contrast between their cold faces and their cool tattoos.
