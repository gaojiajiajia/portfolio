OVERVIEW
This is an tracking app that I design for the animal lovers. Users can drop the pin on the map to record the animals that they meet so they would know which area is their best way to meet all the cute animals again.
INSPIRATION
It is really normal to meet some cute animals but it is not always possible to meet them again. As an animal lovers, what is happier than always knowing where you favourite animals are anytime?
